Fried Chicken is a classic comfort food cherished for its irresistible combination of crispy texture and savory flavor. This dish features pieces of chicken that are seasoned, breaded, and then deep-fried until golden brown and crunchy. Fried chicken is a versatile dish, enjoyed on its own or paired with a variety of sides such as coleslaw, mashed potatoes, or biscuits.
